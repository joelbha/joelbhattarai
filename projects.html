Projects

Capstone

For Joel's senior capstone class he is furthering Dr. Anchal Sareen's research on Unmanned Underwater Vehicle steering. Working on fluid dynamics, control, design, laboratory testing, and data analysis.

Controls

Performed design of PID controllers in time and frequency domain. Designed full-state feedback and compensated full-state feedback controllers. Pictured full-state controller was designed to stabilize an inverted pendulum system with a full-state feedback controller built on Simulink.

Finite Element Analysis

Analyzed a variety of shapes in HyperMesh and OptiStruct. Leveraging 2D and 3D elements, convergence studies, and buckling analysis, to make design recommendations on a variety of systems.
